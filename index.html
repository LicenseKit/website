The LicenseKit SDK can be used to protect your software with commercial licenses. It supports all major Apple platforms (iOS, iPadOS, macOS, tvOS and watchOS) and can validate licenses on-device as well as remotely.

LicenseKit is free to start and affordable to scale for both indies, startups and enterprises. Don't hesistate to reach out if you need help getting started.
